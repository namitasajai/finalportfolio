Section 3 Final Project: Generative Art

Telescope
Section 3 Final Project
In the generative design project using p5.js, my focus was on creating an immersive visual experience. I utilized functions, loops, and conditionals to develop a design that showcased wavy lines in various colors, resembling a landscape with hills. To enhance the interactive aspect, I incorporated an element of randomness or chance into the code, allowing for dynamic variations in the generated patterns. In addition to the design itself, I went a step further and embedded this generative design within a separate website. The website featured user-interactive elements, including a knob on the left side that allowed users to adjust the focus of the image. This interactive control allowed users to play with the visual perspective, adding an extra layer of engagement to the overall experience.
